## Sibling files (one level up in `public/`)

- `vertex_to_region.json`: fsaverage5 vertex → one of our 20 named regions. Approximate vertex-range heuristic; swap for a real Destrieux/Glasser atlas later.
- `vertex_correspondence_fsavg5_to_fsavg6.json`: nearest-neighbor map + 3-nn interpolation weights so a 20484-vertex prediction can be displayed on the higher-resolution mesh.

## Honesty note

Rendering predictions on fsaverage6 is interpolation, not enhanced prediction. TRIBE outputs 20484 cortical samples; what looks like finer activation detail on the hero mesh is barycentric blending of those samples across the denser surface.
